About

What is Kind Regards?

Kind Regards was born out of the idea that if only people could see other parts of the world and form meaningful bonds with people who are not like them, that those experiences and relationships would foster more understanding, tolerance, and a more compassionate society. While travel is a great way for people to expand their horizons, not everyone has the financial ability or the priority to participate in eye-opening travel.

The mission of Kind Regards is to intercept prejudice before it is cemented, and create opportunities for youth to build authentic relationships with people who are different from them. By talking about families, daily life hassles like public transportation versus carpooling with mom, discussing favorite hobbies, and so forth, students will be able to form a picture of a different kind of life, and understand the beauty and value in diverse experiences.

Our mission, in short, is to help raise a generation of compassionate global citizens, one friendship at a time.

What is Kind Regards?

The mission of Kind Regards is to introduce students from different backgrounds and to facilitate conversation—creating bonds and encouraging tolerance and compassion through friendships. The application pulls in quality-of-life data concerning each student’s hometown, and runs a program to pair students of similar ages who’s cities’ quality of life scores differ by a significant margin. Once the pairing is complete, the application provides a messaging platform where students can get to know one another and gain insight about other parts of the world and other people’s life experiences, even if they never have a chance to leave their city.
